Instructions

The Visual Spatial Intelligence Questionnaire consists of multiple-choice questions where you are presented with a 3D object and its rotation. The task involves determining how the object appears after being rotated according to a specified transformation. Each question typically includes:

  1. A reference object: The initial position of a 3D geometric shape or structure.
  2. A rotation prompt: Instructions specifying the axis and degree of rotation (e.g., 90° clockwise around the vertical axis).
  3. Multiple answer choices: Visual representations of the object after the indicated rotation. You must select the correct option.
